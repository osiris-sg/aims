AIMS Field — NFC Scanning Guide

Step-by-step, for field technicians. From tapping a tag to a signed report.
What this app does. You tap your phone on the NFC sticker stuck to a piece of equipment. The app instantly recognises the unit and lets you do one of two things on site: acknowledge a delivery (with proof photos, GPS and a recipient signature) or fill in a Maintenance & Inspection Service Report (checklist, remarks, and two signatures). The very first time a brand-new sticker is tapped, you link it to a product once — after that, every tap goes straight in.
Phone An Android phone with NFC turned on. (iPhone is supported in the installed app only; NFC is not available in the iPhone browser.)
App The AIMS Field app, or open the field site in Chrome on Android.
Login Your AIMS account. Your organisation must have the Field Scan feature enabled by an admin.
Connection Mobile data or Wi-Fi — photos and reports upload to AIMS as you go.

1 Open the app & sign in

Open AIMS Field (or the field web link in Chrome) and sign in with your AIMS account.

  • Field technicians land directly on the scan screen — there's no menu to navigate.
  • If you see a message that "Field scan is not enabled for your organisation", ask an admin to switch it on under Admin → Configuration → Feature Flags.
Tip: Make sure NFC is enabled in your phone settings before you start (Settings → Connected devices → NFC).

2 Tap an asset tag

On the home screen, tap the big Tap to scan button, then hold the back of your phone against the NFC sticker on the equipment until it buzzes.

  • Button text changes to Scanning…, then Looking up… while it finds the unit.
  • If the tag is already linked → you go straight to "What are you doing?" (step 4).
  • If it's a brand-new tag → you go to "New tag" to register it (step 3).
Where's the NFC antenna? On most Android phones it's the upper-middle of the back. Move the phone slowly until it reads.

3 First time? Register the tag

A new sticker isn't linked to anything yet, so you link it once. You don't create a new product — you photograph the nameplate and pick the matching product from the catalog.

3a — Photograph the nameplate. Tap Scan Equipment Label, take a clear photo of the model/serial plate, then tap Analyze. The app reads the model and serial to help you.

3b — Pick the product & confirm. The Product box is pre-filled from the photo; if there's an exact match it's selected for you. Check the Serial number, then tap Create & Bind.

"No matching product"? The product isn't in the catalog yet. Ask the office to add it — new products can only be added from the office, not the field.

4 Choose what you're doing

After a successful tap you see the unit's details and two choices:

  • Start / Acknowledge Delivery — appears only when this unit has an open Delivery Order. The card text changes to match: Start Delivery first, then Acknowledge Delivery after it's been started. If there's no open order it reads "No open delivery order" and is greyed out.
  • Maintenance Service Report — always available; for any service or inspection visit.

Tap Scan another asset at the bottom to go back to the home screen.

5 Delivery: Start → Acknowledge → Sign

This is a two-stage flow with GPS tracking in between, so the office can see the unit move from the depot to the customer.

5a — Start Delivery (at the depot, before you leave). Tap the card → Confirm & Start. GPS tracking begins and a "Tracking delivery" notification stays in your tray.

5b — Acknowledge Delivery (at the destination). Tap the unit's card again → add optional Notes, tap Add photos for proof of delivery, then Continue to signature.

5c — Customer signature. Hand the phone to the recipient, have them sign, optionally type their name, and tap Submit. GPS tracking stops automatically.

Order matters: "Acknowledge" only unlocks after "Start". If a colleague started it, their name shows on the card.

6 Maintenance Service Report — 6 steps

A guided wizard with a progress bar at the top (Step X of 6). Use Back / Next to move; Next stays locked until the current step is complete.

  1. Header — search and pick the Company (required). Client Email auto-fills from their record (editable). Add Job Location. Model and Serial No are filled in for you and can't be edited. Set the Service Date (defaults to today) and an optional Next Service Date.
  2. Checklist — tap each item you checked or serviced (control panel, PLC, HMI, pumps, valves, sensors, pressures, flow rate, …).
  3. Remarks & Time — Time In was captured automatically when you opened the form; Time Out is stamped when you submit. Type your Remarks.
  4. Service signature — shows "SERVICE BY {your company}"; you (the technician) sign. Use Re-sign to redo.
  5. Client signature — the customer reads the certification line, you type their name, and they sign.
  6. Payment — choose one (this submits the report):
    • No Payment Required → the report PDF is emailed to the customer automatically.
    • Payment Required → a Pending Invoice is created on the office dashboard; the office invoices before contacting the customer.

7 Finishing & doing the next one

After you submit (either path), you'll see the Submitted screen confirming it's been sent back to AIMS.

  • Scan another asset — go back to the home screen for the next unit.
  • Back to this asset — return to the same unit's action screen (e.g. to do a second report).
Everything you submitted — photos, signatures, checklist, GPS track — is now visible to the office under Service Reports in the AIMS portal.

8 Troubleshooting

"NFC is required" message Your phone has no NFC or it's off, or you're on an iPhone browser. Turn NFC on in phone settings, or use the installed AIMS Field app on an NFC phone.
Tag won't read Move the phone slowly over the sticker; the antenna is usually upper-middle of the back. Remove thick/metal cases.
"No matching product" when binding The product isn't in the catalog. Ask the office to add it, then tap again.
Delivery card is greyed out There's no open Delivery Order for this unit, or it's already been acknowledged. Use the Maintenance Service Report instead, or check with the office.
"Field scan is not enabled" An admin must enable it under Admin → Configuration → Feature Flags for your organisation.
Next button stays disabled The current step needs something — pick a company, draw a signature, etc. The required field is highlighted.
Photo / signature won't upload Check your data/Wi-Fi connection and try again; the app retries the upload.
Tag bound to the wrong product Don't re-tap to fix it — ask the office to correct the link in the portal.
